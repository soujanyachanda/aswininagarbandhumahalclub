Our Sporting Legacy

Beyond cultural celebrations, we organize annual sports events that energize our community:

  • Competitive tournaments in football and cricket for all age groups
  • Traditional card game championships
  • Special women's sports day promoting female participation
  • Youth development programs nurturing local talent

Social Initiatives

Our commitment to community welfare includes:

  • Free health camps with specialist doctors
  • Annual blood donation drives saving hundreds of lives
  • Winter clothing distribution for the underprivileged
  • Medicine aid programs for elderly community members
  • Educational support for children from low-income families

For over 30 years, we've been serving as the cultural and social heartbeat of Aswininagar, growing from a small neighborhood club to one of the region's most respected community organizations.
