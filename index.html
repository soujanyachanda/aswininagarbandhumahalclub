About Us

Aswininagar Bandhumahal Club is not just an organization—it’s a legacy of devotion, celebration, and social responsibility. Our journey is woven with traditions that bring people together,

with Durga Puja standing as the heart and soul of our club. This grand festival is more than an annual event; it’s a reflection of our cultural pride, artistic excellence, and unwavering spirit of unity. Beyond the festival, we dedicate ourselves to social welfare, cultural programs, and community-driven initiatives, ensuring that our impact goes beyond celebration.

Established in 1992, we continue to embrace our heritage while shaping a future filled with togetherness, progress, and devotion.

Join us in this journey of tradition, unity, and growth!
